Read with Rainbow

Rainbow Reader is the only eReader allowing you to easily switch between users. Each user can have their own settings and libraries. You are also able to create libraries that can be accessed by all users. Yes, you can finally have your cake and eat it too!

Tired of browsing through a library of books you don't care about? Having separate accounts gives you what you need when you need it. Linking your account to the Rainbow will let you access your account on any device and keep your library safe even if something happens to your device.

Parents will be happy to hear that you can create parental accounts. By using these, you can choose exactly what your child can or cannot access. Each parental account can have an unlimited amount of child accounts with different restrictions. This way you can use one device for your whole family. No more need for watching what your child is reading. With Rainbow Reader you can rest assured your child has access to only what you have decided.

You can even set time limitations! You only want your child to use it 1 hour a day? No Problem! You don't want certain accounts to be accessed after 10 on a weekday and 12 on weekends? Done! Mix and match to fit your need!
